Special Needs Community About Us

About Us | Founded 2020

Special Needs Community is a Non-Profit Organization created on September 19th, 2020 with the hope of making a better future for the world. We want to raise a voice in the world to create more awareness about disabilities and special needs. Our goal is to make sure everyone across different communities, societies and groups feel comfortable, included and understood; counting in unique personalities, traits and experiences. We want to bring a positive change, optimism and faith into people's lives.
